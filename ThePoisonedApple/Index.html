Our Story

As a child Onyx Macguire dreamt of a place where they could combine the two things they loved the most: darkness and sweets. As they grew up this dream never died, and they spent their formative years experimenting in the kitchen, making morbid and delicious desserts with their twin sisters. Upon graduating high school they attended Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts in Boulder, Colorado getting their Bachelor's in Baking and Pastry Arts. In 2010 The Poisoned Apple opened in downtown Ash Hollow and has been a go to destination for both dessert connesseur and the darkly inclined ever since.
